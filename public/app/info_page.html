Below is everything you need to know to be successful in the Founders Market Mobile App Ideas Competition for students!

  1. Create a Founders Market account to be eligible to compete in this competition. All applicants are expected to have Founders Market account.
  1. Now grab our attention. Out of all the applications, we will only look at 80 applicants, and this is where the winning idea will be picked from. So, applicants need to attract our attention, and this is how you can do this as an applicant:
    • Like us on Facebook, follow us Twitter and Instagram.
    • Take a screenshot of your Founders Market profile (you can use 'Awesome Screenshot' for your PC/Laptop, or use your mobile phones internal screenshot functionality) and tweet it (mention us), and post it on Instagram with the hashtag #FoundersMarketAppIdeas.
